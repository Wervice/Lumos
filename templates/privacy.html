Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Lumos, as the data controller, collects, uses, and protects your personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and American privacy standards. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your personal information is handled.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

Lumos may collect and process certain personal information for authentication and convenience purposes. The information collected may include:

  • Unique identifiers, such as usernames and passwords, for authentication.
  • Information provided voluntarily by users, such as email addresses or contact details.

Lumos uses the collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and provide access to Lumos services.
  • To enhance user experience and provide convenient features.

2. Cookies

Lumos may use cookies to improve the user experience. Cookies are small files placed on your computer's hard drive that enable Lumos to identify your device and remember certain information.

Lumos uses cookies for authentication and convenience purposes. By default, Lumos does not share these files with third parties. The host of the Lumos copy is solely responsible for the handling and sharing of these files. Cookies can be decrypted by default.

3. Data Sharing

Lumos does not share personal information collected through its services with third parties by default. The responsibility for data sharing lies with the host of the Lumos copy. Lumos will only share personal information when required by law or with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

Lumos takes reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Lumos cannot guarantee absolute security, and you are responsible for taking appropriate measures to protect your personal information.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have certain rights regarding your personal information. These rights include:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal information held by Lumos.
  • The right to restrict or object to the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent previously provided.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Lumos reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to the policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how Lumos is protecting your personal information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or Lumos' data practices, please contact us at privacy@lumos.com.
